# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Use respectful, professional, and evidence-based communication in issues, pull requests, and review discussions.

Examples of expected behavior:

- discuss protocol or persistence tradeoffs directly
- keep review comments technical and specific
- respect operator and user privacy boundaries

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, threats, or insults
- publishing private information without permission
- intentionally disruptive or bad-faith discussion

## Maintainer responsibility

Maintainers may moderate, edit, lock, or close discussions that violate this standard.

## Reporting

If a report should stay private, do not use a public issue. Use a private maintainer contact channel when available.
